Age Verification

Scan the PDF417 barcode on the back of a US driver's license or state ID to check if you are 18 or older.

1. Scan the barcode

Camera is off. Press “Start camera” and hold the back of the ID inside the frame.

2. Result

Demo / proof-of-concept only. This reads the data encoded in the barcode — it does not confirm the ID is authentic or that it belongs to the holder. All processing happens locally in your browser; no data is uploaded.
